Understanding Implied Volatility Around Earnings
TipRanks.com | 2026-04-17
Implied volatility is a key metric in options trading that reflects the market's expectation of a stock's future price movements, particularly around significant events like earnings announcements. High implied volatility typically leads to higher options premiums, as there's a greater perceived chance of a large price swing.
