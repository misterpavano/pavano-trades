Understanding Call and Put Options
T3 Live, London Academy of Trading, Merrill Edge, LuxAlgo, Business Insider | April 15, 2026
Options are contracts that give the buyer the right, but not the obligation, to buy (call option) or sell (put option) an underlying asset at a specific price (strike price) by a certain date (expiration date). Call options profit when the underlying asset's price rises, while put options profit when it falls, offering leverage and tools for both speculation and hedging against market movements.
