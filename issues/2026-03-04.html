Fed Watch
ON HOLD
Stagflation risk rising  |  Fed in difficult spot

ADP Miss + Oil = Fed's Worst Nightmare

The ADP miss (63K jobs, January revised to just 11K) combined with oil-driven inflation creates a genuine stagflation risk — the scenario where the Fed can't cut because inflation is running hot, but can't hike because growth is slowing. Markets had been pricing in 2-3 rate cuts in 2026. Today's data makes that more complicated, not less.

📌 What This Means: The Fed is almost certainly on hold through at least Q2. That's a headwind for long-duration assets (tech, growth) and a tailwind for energy and defensive sectors. If Friday's NFP confirms the ADP weakness, expect a meaningful rate-cut probability reset in fed funds futures.
