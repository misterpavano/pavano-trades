Understanding Volatility Crush Around Earnings
TipRanks.com | April 20, 2026
Volatility crush is a phenomenon where the price of options (known as the option premium) tends to be high in the days leading up to an earnings announcement due to uncertainty. After the earnings are released and the uncertainty is resolved, the option premium typically decreases substantially, even if the stock moves in the expected direction.
